Meet Your Doctor

A Specialist
You Can Trust

Dr. Rekha Goyal is a Gold Medalist BDS graduate from KLE, Karnataka, and holds an MDS in Root Canal Treatment (Endodontics) from Government Dental College, Ahmedabad, along with a Post-Graduate Certificate in Oral Implantology from Pacific, Udaipur. This rare combination of endodontic and implant expertise means even the most complex cases are handled with specialist precision.

At Dr. Rekha Goyal's RB Anjana Multi-Speciality Dental Clinic, every patient receives personalized attention and thoughtful care. Dr. Goyal personally evaluates each case, explains treatment options clearly, and creates customized plans based on individual comfort, needs, and expectations. Rated 4.9★ by over 140 patients, the clinic is known across Freeganj for gentle, painless, and honest treatment at every step.
